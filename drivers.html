Charles Leclerc

Charles Marc Hervé Perceval Leclerc is a Monégasque racing driver, who competes in Formula One for Ferrari. Leclerc was runner-up in the Formula One World Drivers' Championship in 2022 with Ferrari, and has won eight Grands Prix across eight seasons.

Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz Vázquez de Castro is a Spanish racing driver, who is set to compete in Formula One for Williams. Sainz has won four Formula One Grands Prix across 10 seasons. Born in Madrid, Sainz is the son of two-time World Rally Champion Carlos Sainz.
